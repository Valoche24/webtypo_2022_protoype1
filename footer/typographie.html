Boogy Brut is the fusion between Boogy Paper and Bureau Brut. But by the way, who is Boogy Paper? Julien Priez, his real name, is a calligrapher-typographer, member of the collective "High On Type". His work mixes these two disciplines as could his mentors Oscar Ogg and Berthold Wolpe, for whom calligraphy is not necessarily an end but a step in the creative process. It is partly this vision that guided the creation of this family, the ambition being not to render calligraphy in a typography but to create a contemporary digital typography. The result is deliberately sharpened forms that reveal the structure of the original calligraphy, with all imperfections left by the hand, the tool or the paper eliminated. Thus, Boogy Brut is not inspired by a particular model but by a body of research conducted by Julien over the past few years.
